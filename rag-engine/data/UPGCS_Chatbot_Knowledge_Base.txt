SECTION 1: COLLEGE IDENTITY
================================================================================

COLLEGE_NAME: University Post Graduate College, Secunderabad
SHORT_NAME: UPGCS
ALSO_KNOWN_AS: University PG College, OU Secunderabad
AFFILIATED_TO: Osmania University, Hyderabad
TYPE: Co-educational Constituent College
ESTABLISHED: 1947
CITY: Secunderabad
STATE: Telangana
COUNTRY: India
WEBSITE: www.upgcs.in

HISTORY:
- 1947: Founded as "Arts & Science College, Secunderabad" — UG courses only
- 1989: Upgraded and renamed "Post-Graduate College, Secunderabad" — PG courses added
- 2017: Renamed to "University Post Graduate College, OU Secunderabad" (UPGCS)

ABOUT_COLLEGE:
UPGCS is one of the best co-educational institutions of higher learning in the twin cities of Hyderabad and Secunderabad. It is a constituent college of Osmania University. The college offers both Under Graduate (UG) and Post Graduate (PG) courses. Most faculty hold PhD degrees. The college is a preferred destination for foreign students from Sudan, Yemen, Tanzania, Nigeria, Djibouti, Ethiopia and other African countries. The foremost objective is to nurture young minds, stimulate learning and mould their personalities.

ABOUT_UNIVERSITY:
Osmania University (OU) was established in 1918 in Hyderabad, Telangana. It is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in India — the 7th oldest in the country and 3rd oldest in South India. It is UGC-recognised and NAAC-accredited. It governs all academic programmes, examinations, and degree awards for UPGCS.

--- ELIGIBILITY ---

UG_ELIGIBILITY:
- The college is co-educational. Both boys and girls are eligible.
- For B.Com (Computers) and B.Sc (MPC): Must have passed Intermediate (TS Board or equivalent).
- All Intermediate-passed students can apply for B.Com (Computers) regardless of subjects studied.
- For B.Sc (MPC): Minimum 40% in optional subjects required. SC/ST candidates need only pass marks.
- Compartmental/Instant exam candidates eligible if seats are available and documents submitted on time.

PG_ELIGIBILITY:
- Must appear for OUCET (Osmania University Common Entrance Test) / CPGET.
- Admissions through centralised counselling based on OUCET merit.

--- RESERVATIONS ---

LOCAL_SEATS: 85% reserved for local candidates (Osmania University local area)
NON_LOCAL_SEATS: 15% unreserved — open to both local and non-local candidates

CATEGORY_RESERVATIONS:

  BC Group-B — 10%

Q: What is the full name of UPGCS?
A: University Post Graduate College, Secunderabad (also called University PG College, OU Secunderabad).

Q: When was UPGCS established?
A: In 1947, the same year India gained independence.

Q: Is UPGCS a government college?
A: Yes. It is a constituent college of Osmania University, a government university in Hyderabad, Telangana.

Q: Is UPGCS co-educational?
A: Yes. UPGCS is a co-educational institution open to both boys and girls.

Q: What UG courses are offered?
A: BBA, B.Com (Regular), B.Com (Computers), B.Sc (MPC), B.Sc (MPCs), BCA, B.A (HPP), B.A (EPP), B.Sc (Data Science), and B.Sc Honours (Computer Science).

Q: What PG courses are offered?
A: MA English, MA Hindi, MA Economics, MA Public Administration, MA History, MA Political Science, M.Com, MSW, MCA, and M.Sc Data Science.

Q: How do I apply for UG admission?
A: Through DOST — Degree Online Services, Telangana at https://dost.cgg.gov.in/

Q: How do I apply for PG admission?
A: Through CPGET (Common Post-Graduate Entrance Test) at https://cpget.tsche.ac.in/

Q: How do I apply for MCA?
A: Through ICET at https://icet.tsche.ac.in/

Q: What is the eligibility for B.Sc (MPC)?
A: Must have passed Intermediate (TS Board or equivalent) with at least 40% in optional subjects. SC/ST need only pass marks.
